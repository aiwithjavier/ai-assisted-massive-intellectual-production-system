# File Purpose

This document defines the public safety boundaries for the **AI-Assisted Massive Intellectual Production System**.

The purpose is to clarify what can be shared publicly, what should remain private, and how this project should be presented in a responsible, public-safe, recruiter-friendly way.

This file supports future use in:

* GitHub public case study preparation
* CV positioning
* LinkedIn posts
* portfolio content
* interview explanations
* professional public summaries

---

# Core Safety Principle

This project should be presented as a **Human + AI knowledge production system**, not as a release of the actual intellectual property produced by that system.

The public case study may explain the workflow, scale, structure, and professional skills demonstrated.

It should not expose full books, full course materials, proprietary prompts, paid educational content, private methodology, or sensitive human development materials.

# Not Safe to Share Publicly

The following should not be shared publicly:

## Proprietary Content

* full manuscripts
* full chapters
* complete book drafts
* complete paid course scripts
* full student materials
* complete exercises from paid programs
* full slide decks
* full workbooks
* full class notes
* proprietary methodology in full

## Prompt Engineering Assets

Do not publish:

* complete proprietary prompt chains
* internal production prompts
* prompts tied directly to paid content
* prompts containing private strategic context
* prompts revealing complete internal methodology
* prompt libraries not intentionally sanitized
* long-running context prompts used for actual production

## Sensitive Human Development Material

Do not publish:

* therapeutic case examples
* consultante information
* student information
* private personal data
* sensitive psychological or emotional material from real people
* private diagnostic examples
* confidential development notes

## Business and Operational Material

Do not publish:

* commercial strategy
* pricing strategy
* internal launch plans
* private production schedules
* private team management notes
* vendor information
* internal platform logic
* unpublished business models

# Human + AI Responsibility Statement

This project should consistently communicate that AI supports production but does not replace human authorship, responsibility, ethics, judgment, or expertise.

Recommended statement:

The AI-Assisted Massive Intellectual Production System uses AI as a strategic co-pilot inside a human-directed architecture. AI supports ideation, structuring, drafting, rewriting, synthesis, and format transformation, while human judgment preserves meaning, voice, coherence, ethics, and final editorial authority.

# Interview-Safe Explanation

If asked in an interview, the project can be explained as:

I use AI as part of a structured production workflow, not as an autonomous content generator. For large projects, the biggest challenge is not generating text; it is maintaining coherence, voice, structure, purpose, and progression across hundreds of pages, dozens of classes, and multiple formats. My role is to design the architecture, guide the AI, review outputs, preserve consistency, and decide what becomes final.
